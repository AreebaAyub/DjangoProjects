What is the E-CAT Exam?

The Engineering College Admission Test.The E-CAT is conducted by Engineering Universities for admission in BE/BS/BSc engineering degrees.
The E-CAT exam tests examinees on the skills and knowledge.The test duration for E-CAT is almost 2 hours
it include Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry(Can be subsituted by computer science or statistics)
and basic English. It is a key requirement for admission in many top engineering universities of Karachi, Pakistan.
